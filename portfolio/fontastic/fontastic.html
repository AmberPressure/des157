Fontastic: A Web Comic Depicting the Plights of a Young Web Developer

As someone with only a moderate amount of relevant experience in the world of web design, I find that it tends to often be tricky to find a way in.

This series of web-comics is meant to relate to the struggles of your garden variety web developer, whether it be the art of typography changing a website for the better, or even how having conflicting skills can be confusing for a young professional.

That being said, I hope you enjoy this peek into the world of web design, and if you have any questions, feel free to reach out to me via email. Hope you have a great day!
